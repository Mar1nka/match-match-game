Description of the game:

Teach your memory of this game. Your task is to find all pairs of identical images and as quickly as possible. You have 5 minutes. The game has three levels. The more difficult the level, the more items you need to compare. This game will be useful for children and adults to develop their memory, reaction and thinking. Good luck.

    To play:

  • Select two cards to match the pictures;
  • If you match the pictures you can go again;
  • If they do not match, they will turn over;
  • The player that finds all pairs wins!
